Professional Journey

Prema Arunasalam is the founder of the A. Prema & Co. She began her journey as a legal clerk in Messrs. A.G. Kalidas & Co, which provided her with invaluable early exposure to the intricacies of litigation matters. This foundational experience solidified her passion for the law and set the stage for her academic and professional pursuits.

She completed her Bachelor of Law (LL.B Hons) at Multimedia University, where she distinguished herself academically. Eager to deepen her expertise, she later pursued a Master of Law (LL.M) at the prestigious University of Malaya. Prema did her pupillage under the mentorship of Mr. A.G. Kalidas, where she honed her skills by handling a diverse portfolio of cases, including civil litigation, family law, bankruptcy proceedings, and conveyancing matters.

Known for her meticulous approach and client-centric philosophy, Prema is a firm believer in upholding the high ethical standards and integrity of the legal profession. Her leadership has shaped the firm's culture of excellence, ensuring that every client receives personalized legal solutions tailored to their unique needs and feels supported throughout their legal journey.
